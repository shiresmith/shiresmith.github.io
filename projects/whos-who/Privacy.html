Privacy Policy

Whos Who is deeply committed to your right to privacy and takes your privacy seriously. We have very strict privacy policies and we strive to keep your personal information secure. Please note that this Privacy Policy only applies to the Whos Who Products and Services and does not apply to any other websites, software, products or services that you may access from Whos Who, each of which may have privacy policies that are materially distinct from this Privacy Policy.

Collected Information

With your permission, Whos Who may access other personal information on your device, such as your contacts list and details.

Personal information about yourself is not collected or saved in any form.

Cookies are pieces of information that are transferred to your computer from a web server. Our web site does not use or store cookies.

How Collected Information is Used

The information Whos Who collects is used to display information to you on your device. The information is stored only on your device and backups. It is not stored on Whos Who servers or available to any third party.

Who has Access to Collected Information

Collected information is stored locally on your device and your device backups. Only individuals who have physical access to your device can view the information collected and stored by Whos Who.
