Collected Information

With your permission, Whos Who may access other personal information on your device, such as your contacts list and details. If authorized by you, we may also access profile and other information from services like LinkedIn.

Personal information about yourself is not collected or saved in any form.

Cookies are pieces of information that are transferred to your computer from a web server. Most browsers are set up to accept cookies, but you can change your settings to have your browser notify you when you receive a new cookie or to refuse to accept cookies. No personally identifiable information you give to Whos Who will be provided to any third parties for cookie, token or web beacon use.

If authorized by you, we may also access profile and other information from services like LinkedIn. This information may be made available to users through the Whos Who app. Whos Who does not verify and cannot guarantee the accuracy of this public information.

How Collected Information is Used

The information Whos Who collects is used to display information to you on your device. The information is stored only on your device and backups. It is not stored on Whos Who servers or available to any third party.

Who has Access to Collected Information

Collected information is stored locally on your device and your device backups. Only individuals who have physical access to your device can view the information collected and stored by Whos Who.
