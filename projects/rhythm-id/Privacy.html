Privacy Policy

Shiresmith is deeply committed to your right to privacy and takes your privacy seriously. We have very strict privacy policies and we strive to keep your personal information secure. Please note that this Privacy Policy only applies to the RhythmID Products and Services and does not apply to any other websites, software, products or services that you may access from Shiresmith, each of which may have privacy policies that are materially distinct from this Privacy Policy.

Collected Information

With your permission, the RhythmID app will collect and store audio from your device, and store the audio only on your device. This audio is never sent to Shiresmith's servers or otherwise accessible to Shiresmith in any way.

Personal information about yourself is not collected or saved in any form.

Cookies are pieces of information that are transferred to your computer from a web server. Most browsers are set up to accept cookies, but you can change your settings to have your browser notify you when you receive a new cookie or to refuse to accept cookies. No personally identifiable information you give to Shiresmith will be provided to any third parties for cookie, token or web beacon use.

How Collected Information is Used

The information RhythmID collects is used to display information and play audio to you on your device. The information is stored only on your device and backups. It is not stored on Shiresmith's servers or available to any third party.

Who has Access to Collected Information

Collected information is stored locally on your device and your device backups. Only individuals who have physical access to your device can view the information collected and stored by the RhythmID app.
